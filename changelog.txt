Version: 0.0.11
Date: 2024-12-21
  MAJOR UPDATE:
    - THE HUNGER UPDATE!
    - ALL Fruit and ALL Foodtorio edible foods have been configured to fill specific nutrition bars.
    - Independent hunger bar refills from all foods, if hunger hits 0 you take damage.
    - Independent nutrition bars based on the foods you eat. Low nutrition bars do not cause damage.
    - Each full nutrition bar provide 50% multiplier to different stats, to make it worth consuming different foods
    - Simply click a food item from your inventory to open the Eat GUI.
